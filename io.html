Hello, my name is Itunu.

I am a user experience designer inspired by people's stories, how they reason, feel and what their hopes are. Leveraging on data and understanding my team and organization's value is my guiding principle to create products that better enhance the human life.

In 2018 i came across a design prototype which one of my brothers ( a software engineer ) was working on and i couldn't stop asking questions as to how ideas could be transformed to live through design. While working as a Frontend developer at Greenspot Tech i fell in love with design and became a self taught UX/UI designer. Since then i have been designing products across Fin-Tech, eCommerce,Beauty and Food industry.

One thing about me, the beach is my happy place! I am also a podcast addict, few of my favorites are The Next Big Idea, Mixed Methods and School of Greatness.
Oh oh i will be starting my Master degree program in User experience design at Kingston University London this fall.
